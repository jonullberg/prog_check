Privacy Policy

  • Students' names will only be viewable by you, the teacher.
  • ProgCheck reserves the right to review data—excluding students’ names—for the purpose of developing software and features that will benefit the platform and the end-user. This detailed data will not be shared outside of the company, however ProgCheck reserves the right to share anonymized data with developers and prospective clients.
