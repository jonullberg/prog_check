How It Works

  • Prog Check contains sets of 5 or 10 multiple choice questions online
  • Teachers will create student accounts, search for and assign IEP math goals to individual students, and choose between 5 or 10 question tests
  • Teachers can add up to 4 different goals per student
  • Tests are directly related to the assigned goal, as well as tied to a Common Core State Standard
  • Students will continually have ready-to-take tests catered to each of their goals, which teachers can amend at any time
  • Students will log in using their username and student PIN, then take their test(s) online
  • Prog Check randomizes the questiosn on each test so students can take a new test as needed
  • Teachers can log in at anytime to check the progress of each student
  • Prog Check will automatically compile students progress in a phrase that can be used in the Present Levels section of their IEP, as well as in Progress Reports *
